Gen Z's Introduction to Jazz

By Nejma Reza

With the rise of younger pop-jazz vocalists such as Laufey and Faye Webster, many people in Generation Z have expanded their taste from pop, indie, and rock to incorporate jazz in their daily lives.

However, many teens and young adults may be unsure of where to start with this genre. A member of Gen Z reached out to the New York Times, hoping for some insight into their new music.

The New York Times recommends the listener try artists such as:
  • Miles Davis
  • Louis Armstrong
  • Billie Holiday
  • Duke Ellington
