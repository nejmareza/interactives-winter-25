WHO IS OLIVIA DEAN? LEARN MORE ABOUT THE WOMAN WHO WON BEST NEW ARTIST AT 2026 GRAMMY AWARDS

By Nejma Reza

Twenty six year-old artist Olivia Dean won the Grammy for "Best New Artist" on February 1st.

In August of 2026, Dean's single "Man I Need," broke through the charts, reaching the top 100. In an interview with Billboard, she noted that she wasn't initially planning to have this song be a single and she didn't expect it to gain the popularity that it did. In addition to her win, Olivia Dean performed her hit song, "Man I Need" at the the award show.
